Our Facilities

Multi Storied Campus

The campus is located in the heart of the city with nice and furnished infrastructure which is reasonance with the good education atmosphere.

Airy Campus

The classrooms are wide, open and airy where students can feel comfortable and energetic.

Advance Teaching Equipments

The campus is full of advanced equipments which makes the learning intresting and easy.

Qualified and Experienced Teachers

All the team members of NGA highly qualified and experienced which understand the need and expectation of all individual students. They are well experenced in making the students teacher relationship familiar, which in turn enhances better learning.
