ABOUT US

New Generation Academy, NGA was cofounded in 2005 by Mr. S.K. Bajpai and Mrs. Sunita Bajpai with the motto of "VIDYA DADATI VINAYAM". Our main perspective is to guide the child to get prepared for the modern world battle in competition with core Indian culture in his heart, so that he values both modernisation as well as aculturation.

Our curriculum is thoroughly english medium and is good enough to give input to the child with the required from the book as well as practical sessions, so that he is well prepared to get extreme success in society and sustain all kinds of dificulties which may come time to time in his path.

Our Mission

To provide quality education services to strengthen to and open the minds of students in order to culminates to the highest point of success in their lives.

Our Vision

To nurture young minds info fulfilment and realisation of their construction upbringing.

Depth and variety are added each student's life by the school's traditions and accessibility to a board curriculum. The school proudly upholds its commitment of excellence on all fields with emphasis on the motto "VIDYA DADATI VINYAM " ie. Education gives Humility; Humility gives Character; from Character one gets Wealth; from Wealth one gets Righteous (dharmam) life; from Righteousness one gets Happiness.

"Vidya dadati vinayam,

vinaya dadati paatrataam,

paatratva dhanamaapnoti,

dhanat dharmam tatatsukham"
