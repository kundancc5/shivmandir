DISCIPLINE RULES FOR STUDENTS

Following are the rules students must have to follow:

  • Every student should regularly carry with him/her school diary. The school uniform is to be worn on all school days.
  • Students should take care of school property. They should not write or scratch on desks, chair, wall or door of the school nor should they damage the school property.
  • The school is not responsible for the goods or money lost. Money should not be lent or borrowed or articles exchanged.
  • A student who uses unfair means during tests will be given zero in the subject and warning letters. Repetition of the same will result in dismissal.
  • Any student who is found tampering with marks in the progress-report and other school certificate will be asked to leave the school.
  • Students should get their progress report signed by their parents/guardians and return them to the school authorities within three days of receipt.
  • Students are not allowed to use the telephone in school without prior permission of the principal nor will they be allowed to answer telephone calls during class hours. They will not be allowed to receive letter or other mail without prior permission of the principal.
  • Students should always carry the identity cards with them.
  • Students are forbidden to bring crackers, explosive and other dangerous materials to school.
  • Girl students should not wear ornaments. They are not allowed to use nail polish, apply makeup/henna and grow long nails.

GUIDLINES FOR PARENTS

Following are the guidlines for parents:

Our school is for your child. So your child is a Very Important Person in our school. Our aim is to make him/her fit for life. Your maximum co-operation is essential to bring out the best in the child.

  • Parents are requested to sign the progress reports, answer papers and teacher's remarks in the school diary and return them within the prescribed time.
  • The participation of the father and the mother/guardian on the occasion of the school anniversary and seminars conducted by the PTA and the School authorities is compulsory.
  • Parents are requested to pay the fees regularly and to keep the fee receipts till the end of the academic year.
  • To the best interest of their children, parents are requested to respond immediately when they are asked to call on the school authorities and teachers.
  • Parents are expected to show due respect to the school authorities and teachers.
  • Parents are expected to point out any mistake committed by the school in black and white, to the respective school authorities without any hesitation in order to achieve the full objectives of our School.
  • If you have any complaint about the services rendered, please Inform the Principal/Managing Director in writing.
